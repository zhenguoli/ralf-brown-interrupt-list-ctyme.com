ENVLOCK - INSTALLATION CHECK

AX = 6262h
SI = 1994h


Return:
AX = 1994h if installed
ES = ENVLOCK's resident segment

Notes: To deactivate ENVLOCK, zero out the byte at ES:[0102h]

Program: ENVLOCK is a TSR by Alexander Yanovsky that forces other TSRs to deallocate their environment when they stay resident

Category: Resident Utilities - Int 21h - E
