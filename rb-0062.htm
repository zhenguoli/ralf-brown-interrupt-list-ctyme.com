IRQ6 - DISKETTE CONTROLLER

Desc: This interrupt is generated by the floppy disk controller on completion of an operation

Notes: Default handler is at F000h:EF57h in IBM PC and 100%-compatible BIOSes. May be masked by setting bit 6 on I/O port 21h. DOS 3.3+ revectors IRQ6 to a stack-switching routine unless STACKS=0 has been set in CONFIG.SYS. MS/PC-DOS use the IBM Interrupt Sharing Protocol (see #02568) when hooking this IRQ
