HUNTER 16 - SPLIT DISPLAY

AH = 66h
AL = control bits (see #00187)
BH = top row of lower section of LCD (0..7)
BL = top row of lower section of the virtual screen (0..24)


Return:
Nothing

Desc: Split the LCD screen horizontally into two parts; each part may be individually scrollable or fixed.

Note: The Hunter 16 has a 240x64 LCD display which serves as a window into a 640x200 virtual screen 


Bitfields for HUNTER 16 control bits:

Bit(s)  Description     (Table 00187)

0-1    01:
No split, 10: allow split
2      allow scrolling the upper part
3      allow scrolling the lower part

Category: Video - Int 10h - H
