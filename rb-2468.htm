C&T SuperState BIOS - GET AUTO WAKE UP DATE

AH = FDh
BL = 04h


Return:
CH = year century in BCD
CL = year low in BCD
DH = month in BCD
DL = date in BCD

See Also: AH=FDh/BL=02h - AH=FDh/BL=05h

Category: Vendor-specific BIOS Extensions - Int 1Fh - C
