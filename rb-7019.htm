FGDRIVER v4.02 - FG_MEASURE - GET DELAY UNITS PER CLOCK TICK

AX = 0070h


Return:
AX = delay units per clock tick (processor-dependent)

Notes: The FGDRIVER functions are rearranged with each major release, but their parameters do not change (see #03474 at AX=0000h). These delay units are used by "FG_STALL" (see AX=00BFh)

See Also: AX=00BFh
